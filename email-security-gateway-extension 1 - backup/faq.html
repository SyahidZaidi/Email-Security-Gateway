Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add spam words or threat keywords?

Go to the Settings page, enter the words or domains you want to filter, and they will be saved for future scans.

How does email encryption work?

The email encryption feature uses AES encryption to ensure your email contents are secure and private.

Why aren't filtered emails showing up?

Ensure you have enabled spam and threat detection in the Settings. If the issue persists, contact support.

Can I turn off email filtering?

Yes, you can toggle the spam and threat filters directly from the Settings page.

What should I do if I forget my secret key?

For security purposes, keys are not stored by the extension. Regenerate a new key and re-encrypt your message.
